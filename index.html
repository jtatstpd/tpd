Brand

Brand Statement

A well developed Personal Brand can be boiled down into one brief statement.

The purpose of this brand statement is to help others understand what ‘Thornton Daily’ does, why it does it, and what value it provides.

I’m Thornton Daily - A professional product designer who's obsessed with helping people develop their productivity toolbox. From the newest tech and apps to improve peoples workflow to useful tips to break bad habits I share anything and everything productivity.

Brand Values

These 3 Core Values should be a guide when producing content. All content should embody these 3 values.

Practical

The advice that is given on Thornton Daily’s platforms should be applicable by anyone who is excited to put effort towards being more productive.

Straightforward

Content should be to the point and avoid any unecessary fluff or f rills. High Value content will by its nature spark interest. In the instance theatrics are needed to make content engaging - re-evalute the content.

Consistency

towards bettering ones self. In a similar manner content should be distributed regularly.

Logo

Guidelines

It's necessary to protect the integrity of this logo at all times.

As such, we have guidelines to provide clear instruction on how to use Thornton Daily’s logo in a variety of contexts.

Protecting our Logo

Clear Space

This grid represents the minimum space required for separating the logo f rom competing elements.

Ensuring proper use of our logo is important to convey a professional, consistent, and trusted brand image.

Always allow half the total height of the primary icon represented as clear space around the icon, and no additional elements should exist within that space.

Always allow half the total height of the secondary icon represented as clear space around the icon, and no additional elements should exist within that space.

Color

Color Palette

To help achieve brand recognition, it is important that our color palette is applied consistently.

Out Primary brand colors are coral, white, and grey These colors are meant to be modern, bold and inviting.

Use dark grey & blacks, white or light grey and never alter the shade or saturation of coral. Grey and white can be altered for platform specific application.
